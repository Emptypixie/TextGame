Title Screen commands

  • "play": Starts the game.
  • "option": Show opptions.
  • "help": Show help.

In-game commands:

  • numbers: When you see a list of choices, you can type in a number to select the choice. Or you could write the whole phrase.
  • "room": Display monsters in the room.
  • "stat" or "stats": Display player stats.
  • "fight": Fight monsters in the room.
  • "map": Display map around the player.
  • arrow keys: Move to the room in that direction.
  • "look" + [monster name]: Examine a monster
  • "quit" or "exit": Exit game

During combat

  • "attack" or "a": Perform offensive attack.
  • "skill" or "spell" or "s": Use skill or spell
  • "run away" or "run": Try to run away.
